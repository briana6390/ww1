1/31/1918 (Thursday)

Military mailed a letter to you.

In 1/18/1918, We mailed you a letter about you are recruited to the military. We were writing this letter to you because we didn't hear any response from you. We are very concerned about you and hope you did receive this letter. If you receive the letter, please come to the military or we will find you.
